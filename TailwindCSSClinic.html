About Us

Committed to Healing,
Driven by Innovation

Founded in 2001, MediCore has grown from a community clinic to a leading multi-specialty hospital serving thousands of patients annually. We combine evidence-based medicine with the latest clinical technology to deliver outcomes that matter.

Our team of over 85 board-certified specialists works across 18 departments, supported by state-of-the-art diagnostics, a modern surgical suite, and a dedicated intensive care unit — all unified through our integrated clinical management platform.
